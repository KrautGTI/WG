About Me

About Me!

Hey there! I'm William Gard, and I'm on a mission to turn strategic communication into a captivating puzzle that leaves everyone wanting more. Armed with a degree in Strategic Communication, I'm stepping into the world of strategic communication with a twinkle in my eye and a passion for making messages click.


Why Strategic Communication?

Imagine communication as a puzzle board with scattered pieces waiting to connect. I'm that person who loves piecing it all together. Strategic communication isn't just about words; it's about crafting a narrative that resonates and sparks curiosity. I'm ready to dive in and create those "aha" moments.


Experiences that Define Me:

Just like a puzzle, my academic journey and projects have been pieces that shaped me. From collaborative multimedia campaigns to diving into research for strategic insights, I've found joy in every piece of the communication puzzle.


What Sets Me Apart:

I'm not your typical communicator—I'm someone who finds joy in the puzzle. Beyond the theories, I bring a passion for unraveling challenges, a love for storytelling, and a commitment to making every piece count.


Puzzle Breaks and Brain Teasers:

Outside the world of communication, you'll often find me taking a break with a challenging puzzle or brain teaser. It's my way of staying sharp, fostering creativity, and having a bit of fun in the process.

Lets Connect!

If you're looking for a communicator who brings a touch of puzzle magic to the strategic table, I'm your person! Let's connect, share a few laughs, and explore how we can create some communication masterpieces together.
